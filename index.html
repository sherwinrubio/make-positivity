Who I am

Hi! My name is Sherwin and I want to share two things that I am deeply passionate about; People and Learning. I have put these two passions to use while working in the publishing industry for over 10 years. I started off as a Cashier, but I quickly worked my way up to becoming one of the Lead Solutions Architects for one of the largest publications in New York!

I managed to get recognized early on by delivering insurmountable value, making a positive impact on the people and company I work for. I have since then come to realize that providing value to others is the key to success.

My goal is to share all the practical knowledge I have gained by studying about developing emotional intelligence, the art of learning, navigating corporate culture, and more!
